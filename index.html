Team

Our team assembles a unique set of profiles combining Japanese and ASEAN very senior experience in Fintech, IT & data science, asset management, investment bank, PE, companies, strong ability to understand ASEAN cultures and deep local network for sourcing pipelines and developing business. For examples, we have members from Japan, Indonesia, Mongolia and other countries having global experience / education background.

Hitoshi Sugamoto

Most recently he was one of the most successful private bankers at Goldman Sachs in Tokyo and previously a managing director at Wealth Management team at Deutsche Bank Group where he created strong relationship with more than 200 HNWIs in Japan. Until 2004 he successively held position of a branch manager at 4 major retail offices at Nikko securities after 7 year experience of retail sales at Nikko group. During this period as a head of business strategy planning team he led successful M&A transactions took place between Nikko vs. CITI in 1999, and Nikko vs. SMBC in 2009.

Takeshi Kurosawa

28 years investment experience in investment banking and asset management. Since 2007, Takeshi has been primarily involved in private equity investments in Mongolia and Bangladesh. In 2007, he helped set up the first offshore investment bank Frontier LLC in Mongolia. In 2014, he launched an impact investment fund for Bangladesh PEs at Osiris Group in Hong Kong. Previously, he was head of IR at hedge funds in Tokyo (2003 - 2008) and a strategist on the equity program trading team at Salomon Brothers Asia, Citigroup and NRI (1990 - 2003). BS in Physics, Keio University; MS in Applied Physics, Tokyo Institute of Technology.

Yuji Iwasaki

More than 30 years of experience in the investment banking and corporate finance industries in Tokyo and Zurich, primarily with Yamaichi Securities. He has led fund-raising activities for more than 300 Japanese and Asian enterprises over the course of his career. After Yamaichi he served as CFO of three listed companies in Japan. In 2008, he was invited to Mongolia to work as COO of Frontier Securities. In 2012, he set up his own company, Strategic Partners in Mongolia. BS in Structural Architecture at Tokyo Metropolitan University and received a certificate of BA from Diavox Collage in Lausanne, Switzerland. He has 1st Class Chartered Architect qualification in Japan.

Adrian Emmanuel Haryo Seno
