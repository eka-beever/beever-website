What Does “beever” Offer?

We are PT Beever System Indonesia, based Fintech start-up established in 2016 developing innovative contents “beever”.

We focus on life of unbanked people highly populated in frontier economies where they tend to own more mobile phones than before as central tools for cashless payment as well as communication.

We think deeply about how to contribute to changing their life safer and more efficient in post-coronavirus era. Our solution is to provide digital eMoney platform that is operatable in your hands without going to bank ATM. Anytime anywhere you can top-up salary and access to various functions of both payment settlements and digital financial products.

We also implement B2B2C operation where the credit risk among employee is guaranteed by company, and we ally with successful conglomerates who well understand local as well as global capital market which helps us do quicker marketing and more strategic operation.

We wish "beever" could be a paradigm shifter in the world of cashless payment among unbanked people.

First signing took place on July 28, 2021 with Koperasi Sumber Jaya at Karawang, which was followed by the signing with 8 other Koperasi/ companies.


This type of business with Koperasi (and companies) is quite unique. Koperasi members (company employees) use Koperasi to buy daily commodity and foods for their life. After they have spent all the salary, they still need those goods every day.

This is the key to our business. We provide them our service to buy daily commodity and foods after they have spent all the salary. They have to use our Salary Advance service without any choice. Therefore the usage rate is very high. According to our recent data, 80% of registered employees use our service repeatedly. We expect total 290,000 employees use our service through this Koperasi scheme.

By connecting salary with payment settlement in eMoney platform, AID expects to be able to add more degrees of freedom on payment among employees who wish to get salary paid and pay settlement anytime, anywhere, and add more value to the relationship between employers and employees who use our eco-system platform.

Fund Raising through Issuing of Convertible Notes

December 29, 2020

Asia Impact Development Pte. Ltd. (“AID") hereby announces that its Board of Directors resolved to issue convertible notes through third party allotment as follows:

Final closing date : December 31, 2020
Total amount issued : JPY 110,000,000
Conversion price : USD 35 per 1 ordinary share
Maturity : 2 years after closing
Number of new shares issued upon full conversion : 30,179 shares

If new shares are not issued less than US$35.00 per share before the maturity or full conversion of this convertible notes.


Reason of fund raising
AID has been developing Indonesian market for about 2 years for its Salary Advance Payment Mechanism. After it has been successfully launched its first operation in Indonesia in July, 2020 with Salim Group, it is now expanding its business for Financial Aggregator Business. AID is also pursuing opportunity to acquire a P2P company. Together with those competitive characteristics, AID is now cultivating other Salim Group companies and other groups outside of Salim Group. The capital increase enhances the speed of further development of its business in Indonesia


Business Model and Projection
Salary Advance Payment Mechanism is a quite new business model; Employees are able to withdraw their salary in advance through eMoney directly top-up to their accounts to transfer, make shopping, paying bills and so on. This is a system which will let those Employees and their families to focus on their job without economic concern. Financial Aggregator Business provides additional services to Employees for their asset building and welfare benefit. Those services include, but not limited to; asset management and insurance. Company (employer of Employees) may subsidize those services to retain and hire talented Employees
